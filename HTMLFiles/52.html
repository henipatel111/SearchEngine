Facebook Mobile and Desktop Apps

You can use Facebook to stay connected on many devices including iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows and feature phones. To see what's new with the app and install the latest version, you can go to the Facebook for Mobile page or visit your phone's app store (ex: iTunes App Store, Google Play Store, Windows Store).
